Got Questions?

Schengen Visa FAQs

Everything you need to know about applying for a Schengen Tourist Visa from the UK.

What is a Schengen Tourist Visa and who needs one from the UK?
A Schengen Tourist Visa allows you to travel freely across 26 European countries for up to 90 days within any 180-day period. After Brexit, UK citizens no longer have automatic right to live or work in the EU, but can still visit visa-free for short trips. However, some nationalities residing in the UK (e.g. Pakistani, Indian, Bangladeshi passport holders) DO require a Schengen visa. Swift Trip Holidays assists all UK residents who need a Schengen Tourist Visa.
How long does Schengen visa processing take from UK?
Standard processing typically takes 5–15 working days from the date of biometrics submission. We recommend applying at least 4–6 weeks before your intended travel date. Our fast-track advice service helps you apply early and correctly, reducing delays.
What documents do I need for a Schengen Tourist Visa?
Key documents include: valid passport (with at least 3 months beyond travel date), completed visa application form, 2 recent passport photos, travel insurance (minimum €30,000 cover), bank statements (last 3–6 months), proof of employment or business, hotel/accommodation booking, return flight itinerary, and proof of UK residency. We provide a personalised checklist for your specific country application.
What is the Schengen visa fee in 2024/2025?
The official Schengen visa fee is €90 (approximately £77) for adults and €45 for children aged 6–12. Children under 6 are exempt. This is the consulate fee paid directly. Our service fee is separate and covers document review, application preparation, appointment guidance and full support. Contact us for our current service pricing.
Can I apply if I've been refused a Schengen visa before?
Yes! A previous refusal does not ban you from applying again. However, you must disclose previous refusals honestly and address the reasons for refusal. Our experts analyse your previous refusal letter and build a stronger, more compelling application. Many of our clients with prior refusals have been successfully approved.
How do I book an appointment at the visa application centre?
We help you navigate the visa application centre (VFS Global, TLSContact, BLS International etc.) booking system. We advise on the earliest available appointments, assist with online booking, and prepare you for what to bring on the day. Appointments can be scarce — applying early with our guidance maximises your chances.
